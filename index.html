What We Do

Our work is to help the African society in the following aspects

Education

We raise awareness to university students about self-employee when are at university and after graduating. Because more than 75% of the university students do not get formal jobs after finishing the university. We are going to rise their talents and buisness ideas in order to combuct the unemployment rate. To support our work, visit our DONATE page

Charity

"we don't need to have deep pockets or be rich to help the needy, the poor and the hungry, we need to have heart" said Kevin Dcruz. Through our NGO we will be helping the special groups such as street children, aged people who are in special camps, disabled, people who can not afford paying their health treatments, students who cannot afford their school fees etc. Our charity tours will be conducted quartly a year. For charity donations, visit our DONATE page

Experience sharing

On our blog page, we will be posting different stories of African youth so as to inspire, educate, entertain, rising awareness or life experience. This is for anyone who wants to share his/her story with us. Also, sharing your story with others, is an amazingly powerful way of healing yourself. It helps you to grasp the full meaning of something that happened in the past. Visit our BLOG for more informations.
