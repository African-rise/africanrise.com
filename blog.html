My story

Hi, my name is Savio Kalinga. I am the founder and the C.E.O of African Rise NGO. When I was in advanced level, me and my group members, we did a project which goes by the following title "Problems hindering female students to pass with sufficient grades in National Examinations and their solutions". Since I was in advanced level and it was single sex (boys only), then we asked for permission to do it at ordinary level students. We did our project well and we got a lot of problems such as that faces most female students. After we did the project, personally I thought if I can use my ability to solve their problems and make most of female students attain their goals through education. Then, the idea of starting this NGO was generated in my mind. Not only of that reason but there are many more reasons which I will share with you through this page. See you on my second story.
